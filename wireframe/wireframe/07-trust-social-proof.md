# 07. Trust & Social Proof (Conversion Reinforcement)

## Purpose

This section reinforces credibility and reduces last-mile conversion friction.

It supports users who:
- Scroll to validate trust before purchase
- Need reassurance after reviewing specs and FAQs
- Are comparing alternatives in multiple tabs

---

## SEO & CRO Role

While not directly keyword-driven, this section:
- Improves engagement and time on page
- Reduces pogo-sticking behavior
- Supports overall page quality signals

---

## Trust Signals to Include

**Primary signals:**
- Verified customer reviews
- Aggregate star ratings
- Usage metrics (e.g. “Trusted by 50,000+ users”)

**Secondary signals:**
- Press mentions
- Industry certifications
- Awards or recognitions

**Tertiary signals:**
- Secure checkout badges
- Warranty and return guarantees
- Customer support availability

---

## Structural Patterns

**Recommended layouts:**
- Horizontal trust bar (logos, ratings)
- Review summary with expandable details
- Short testimonial carousel

**Placement:**
- Below FAQs
- Near final CTA on long PDPs

---

## Content Guidelines

- Use concise, scannable elements
- Prefer data-backed claims over adjectives
- Avoid exaggerated marketing language

**Good:**  
“4.6 ★ average from 3,200 verified reviews”

**Avoid:**  
“Best product ever made”

---

## Example (Placeholder)

**Trusted by thousands of customers worldwide**  
4.6 ★ average rating from over 3,200 verified buyers.

**What customers are saying:**  
“Battery life exceeded expectations, even on long flights.”  
“Comfortable enough to wear all day without fatigue.”

**Shop with confidence**  
- 30-day hassle-free returns  
- 1-year manufacturer warranty  
- Secure checkout

---

## Why This Section Matters

Trust signals:
- Address emotional blockers to conversion
- Reinforce decisions already made rationally
- Increase conversion without increasing traffic
